Welcome to the Free Food Map Project

Free Food Map connects people with food banks, pantries, churches, and community kitchens. The project combines structured data, public suggestions, and admin review so people can find nearby food resources faster and with more confidence.

Please call a location before visiting. Hours and food availability can change quickly.
