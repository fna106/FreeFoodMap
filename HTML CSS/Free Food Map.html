Welcome to the Free Food Map Project

Free Food Map connects people with accurate, up-to-date resources like food banks, churches, and community kitchens. Started by Penn State students and built with the help of a partner university in France, we combine global teamwork with local impact. We verify our listings in real-time so you can find reliable support instantly. Together, we are building a safer world where no one has to go hungry.
